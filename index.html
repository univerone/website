What is MLModelCI?

MLModelCI provides multimedia researchers and developers with a one-stop platform for efficient machine learning (ML) services.
The system leverages DevOps techniques to optimize, test, and manage models. It also containerizes and deploys these optimized and validated models as cloud services (MLaaS).
You just need to register your models to our platform and it will take over the rest tasks..
